Bilbo Baggins

I was a simple Hobbit. Just having my breakfats, second breakfast, elevenses, lunch, afternoon tea, dinner, and supper.
Of course, enjoying the use of my pipe throughout the day as well. Imerely lived my quiet, humble life until...
An adventurous wizard requested my help. Thus, bringing me to my true beginning of my life.
I may not be considered as a normal, respectable hobbit, due to my leaving the Shire to fill my memories with fun and adventure,
but I do not regret any of the choices.
